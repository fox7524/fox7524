## 📖 Comprehensive Profile

I operate on the principle that the most profound innovations occur at the intersection of disparate disciplines. I do not wait for higher education to begin building; I am actively contributing to hardware, software, and media development today.

### 💻 Software Architecture & Information Security
As a full-stack developer, I construct robust backend logic and scalable architectures. My current research involves deploying and fine-tuning localized Large Language Models (LLMs), such as Qwen and DeepSeek, to optimize specialized workflows. Concurrently, my foray into cybersecurity informs my development practices, ensuring secure, resilient infrastructure. My primary development environment is a macOS system powered by the M5 Pro silicon—leveraging its paradigm-shifting architecture, which eliminates traditional efficiency cores in favor of absolute performance and super-core computing for intensive machine learning compilation and rendering tasks.

### ⚙️ Advanced Prototyping & Hardware Engineering
Rooted in the maker ethos, I orchestrate the seamless integration of digital logic and physical mechanics. My proficiency encompasses low-level programming of the ESP32-S3 (specifically utilizing the N8R2 architecture), intricate PCB design, and complex 3D printing methodologies. My competitive tenure includes engineering sophisticated, autonomous maze-solving robots and securing the prestigious Control Award alongside an 8th-place finish at the MEM Robotics Competition with an advanced FRC-style chassis.

### 🚀 Aerodynamics & UAV Development
My tenure as the technical lead of the Üsküdar UAV Team at BİLSEM cemented my foundational knowledge of flight dynamics and telemetry. My design process relies heavily on rigorous aerodynamic simulations executed via XFLR5 and OpenVSP. My autonomous and remote-controlled hardware portfolio includes the engineering of 8 RC aircraft, complex satellite prototypes, and the synthesis of solid rocket propellants.

### 🎥 Cinematic Production & Visual Design
Operating at the intersection of engineering and art, I am an accomplished graphic designer, colorist, and filmmaker. Collaborating closely with my production partners, Efe Yavuz and Kutay, we have executed critically acclaimed projects such as *Yitik Zamanın İzinde*. I possess an intimate, mechanical understanding of my optical equipment—navigating the specific, nonlinear focal resistance of the Canon 18-55mm DC III lens between 18-24mm with precision—and execute high-fidelity post-production utilizing DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, and Blender.
